About

Vastu Vihar is committed to providing superior quality construction through unmatched project management while maintaining the highest level of authenticity in every aspect of a business. Vastu Vihar Techno culture was established in the year 1994, co-sponsored by HUDCO and BMTPC of India with a motto to hold ourselves to a higher standard and ultimately provide a better housing experience. Vastu Vihar is the leading construction group of Eastern India, operating in 45 cities of Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha, West Bengal and Uttar Pradesh. Primarily engaged in building low-cost residential units with a staff of 1700 employees, the company has completed 20,000 projects of construction out of 40,000 projects and 20,000 are under process. We offer clients the accessibility and support of a local firm with the stability and resources of a multi-national organization.
